Creative Use Of Space Sound

Space-related movies often utilize sound in unique ways to capture the vastness, isolation, and mystery of the cosmos. Since space is a vacuum where sound cannot travel, filmmakers must creatively design their soundscapes to enhance the story while maintaining scientific accuracy. Films like Gravity and Interstellar are particularly notable for their innovative use of sound and silence.These films use sound to immerse audiences in the awe and danger of space exploration, balancing realism with cinematic artistry.
