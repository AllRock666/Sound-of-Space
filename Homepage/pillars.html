Pillars of Creation

The “Pillars of Creation” are a stunning feature of the Eagle Nebula, known for their dramatic columns of gas and dust where new stars are born. The sonification of this image translates the visual data into sound by moving horizontally across the image from left to right. This process allows the sonification to capture both optical and X-ray light in an auditory format.

In this sonification, the vertical position of the light data controls the pitch, varying across a continuous range. The structure of the pillars is emphasized with sweeping pitches that rise and fall, mirroring the towering columns of the nebula. Listeners can enjoy the two distinct "melodies" from optical and X-ray light either individually or simultaneously, offering a rich and immersive auditory experience of this cosmic phenomenon.

Sonification credits: SYSTEM Sounds (M. Russo, A. Santaguida)
